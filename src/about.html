About DreamPrize11 - Cricket Prediction Game

Welcome to DreamPrize11, the ultimate destination for all cricket enthusiasts who love predicting match outcomes! Our cricket prediction game is designed to test your knowledge and instincts while giving you a chance to win exciting rewards. With DreamPrize11, you can take your passion for cricket to the next level and earn points by accurately predicting the outcome of upcoming cricket matches.

How DreamPrize11 Works:

1. Sign Up & Login:

To get started, create a DreamPrize11 account by providing a username, email address, and password. Once you have successfully registered, log in to your account and access our exciting prediction game.

2. Choose a Match:

Navigate to the 'Upcoming Matches' section to find a list of cricket matches that are scheduled to take place in the near future. Select the match you wish to predict the outcome for, and proceed to the next step.

3. Predict & Choose an Option:

For each selected match, you will be presented with a question related to the outcome of the game. This could be about the winning team, total runs scored, or the highest wicket-taker, among other possibilities. Carefully assess the question and choose the option that you believe is the most likely outcome.

4. Earn Points & Compete:

Once you have submitted your prediction, watch the match to see if your choice was accurate. If your prediction is correct, you will be awarded points. Your total points will accumulate over time, allowing you to climb the DreamPrize11 leaderboard and compete against other cricket enthusiasts.

5. Redeem & Enjoy Rewards:

As you accumulate points, you will become eligible to redeem them for various rewards, including merchandise, discount vouchers, and exclusive experiences. Visit the 'Rewards' section on the DreamPrize11 website to explore the available options and redeem your points.

Rules & Guidelines:

  • You must be at least 18 years of age to participate in the DreamPrize11 cricket prediction game.
  • Users are allowed to submit only one prediction per match.
  • Predictions must be submitted before the scheduled start time of the match.
  • Points will be awarded based on the accuracy of the prediction and the difficulty of the question.
  • In case of a tie in points, the winner will be determined by the date and time of their last correct prediction.
  • The leaderboard will be updated regularly to reflect users' points and rankings.
  • DreamPrize11 reserves the right to modify or discontinue the prediction game at any time without prior notice.

Conclusion:
